Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you contact us through our website, join our organization, or participate in our theatre activities, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Age and demographic information for event planning
  • Theatre experience and skills for role assignments
  • Emergency contact information for safety purposes
  • Payment information for event fees (if applicable)

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we may automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited for website improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Communication about upcoming theatre programs, events, and workshops
  • Event registration and participation coordination
  • Role assignments and rehearsal scheduling
  • Emergency contact during events and activities
  • Organizational record-keeping and membership management
  • Improving our services and website functionality
  • Compliance with legal obligations and safety requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Event Coordination: With other participants for rehearsals and performance coordination
  • Venue Requirements: With performance venues for security and safety purposes
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government authorities
  • Emergency Situations: To protect the safety and well-being of participants
  • Service Providers: With trusted service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the purpose:

  • Active Members: Information is retained while you are an active participant
  • Former Members: Contact information may be retained for up to 3 years for alumni communications
  • Event Records: Performance and event records may be kept for historical and promotional purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some information may be retained longer as required by applicable laws

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure storage of physical and digital records
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular review and update of security practices
  • Secure communication channels for sensitive information
  • Training for organization members on data protection

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another organization
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Cookies and Website Technologies

Our website may use the following technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website (anonymized data)
  • Language Preferences: To remember your language selection

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

We welcome participants of all ages in our theatre activities. For participants under 18 years:

  • Parental or guardian consent is required for participation
  • We collect only necessary information for safe participation
  • Parents/guardians have the right to review and request deletion of their child's information
  • Special care is taken to protect minors' privacy and safety

9. Photography and Media

During our theatre activities and performances:

  • Photos and videos may be taken for promotional and documentation purposes
  • Participation consent includes agreement to such documentation
  • You may request removal from promotional materials
  • Special consent is obtained for commercial use of images

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We will post the updated policy on this page
  • We will notify active members via email or other communication methods
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Continued participation constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
